Privacy Policy

RepTrack Pro

Last updated: July 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

RepTrack Pro collects and stores your workout data locally on your device. We may access your Apple Health data with your explicit permission to:

  • Read workout and health data for insights and analytics
  • Write workout data to Apple Health to track your fitness progress

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide workout tracking and fitness analytics
  • Generate personal records and progress reports
  • Sync with Apple Health (with your permission)

3. Data Storage and Security

All your workout data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers. Your data is protected by iOS security measures.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with Apple HealthKit to provide enhanced fitness tracking. Apple Health data is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Export your data at any time through the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Revoke HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@reptrackpro.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
