RepTrack Pro

Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: July 30, 2025

🔒 Your Privacy Matters: RepTrack Pro is designed with privacy at its core. All your workout data stays on your device, and we never collect or share your personal information without your explicit consent.

1 Information We Collect

RepTrack Pro operates on a privacy-first principle. The information we access includes:

  • Workout Data: Exercise routines, sets, reps, weights, and workout duration
  • Health Metrics: Body weight, height, and fitness goals (stored locally)
  • Apple Health Data: Only with your explicit permission to read/write workout and health data
  • App Usage: Basic analytics to improve app performance (no personal data)

2 How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to enhance your fitness experience:

  • Track your workout progress and generate personal records
  • Provide detailed analytics and insights about your fitness journey
  • Sync workout data with Apple Health (only with your permission)
  • Create personalized workout recommendations based on your history
  • Generate progress reports and achievement milestones

3 Data Storage and Security

🛡️Local Storage: All your personal workout data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

  • No Cloud Storage: We do not transmit your personal workout data to external servers
  • Device Security: Your data is protected by iOS security measures and device encryption
  • Data Isolation: Your information cannot be accessed by other apps without permission
  • Backup Protection: Data included in encrypted iOS backups only

4 Third-Party Integrations

RepTrack Pro integrates with the following services to enhance your experience:

  • Apple HealthKit: Read and write workout data (requires explicit permission)
  • Apple Calendar: Schedule workout sessions (optional integration)
  • Crash Analytics: Anonymous crash reports to improve app stability

All third-party integrations respect your privacy choices and Apple's privacy guidelines.

5 Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Data Export: Export all your workout data in standard formats anytime
  • Individual Workout Deletion: Delete specific workouts from your history whenever you choose
  • Complete Data Removal: Use the "Clear All Data" button in Settings to permanently remove all workout data
  • App Uninstallation: Delete all data by uninstalling the app from your device
  • Permission Control: Revoke HealthKit and other permissions in iOS Settings
  • Selective Sharing: Choose which data to share with Apple Health
  • Offline Mode: Use the app completely offline if preferred

🗑️Data Management: RepTrack Pro gives you granular control over your workout data. Delete individual sessions or clear everything at once - it's entirely your choice.

6 Children's Privacy

RepTrack Pro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app
  • You will be prompted to review updated terms when necessary
