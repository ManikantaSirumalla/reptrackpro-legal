Create Your Profile

  1. Launch the App
    Open RepTrack Pro on your iPhone or iPad. If this is your first time, you'll see the welcome screen.
  2. Set Up Your Profile
    Tap "Get Started" and fill in your information:
    • Your name and age
    • Height and weight

    • Experience level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)

  4. You're Ready!
    Start tracking your first workout or generate an AI-powered fitness plan.

Start a Workout

  1. Navigate to Workouts Tab
    Tap the "Workouts" tab at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Choose Your Workout
    You can:
    • Start a quick workout (add exercises on the fly)
    • Use a workout template (Push/Pull/Legs, Full Body, etc.)
    • Start from an AI-generated plan
  3. Add Exercises
    Tap "Add Exercise" to browse the exercise library or search for specific exercises. Select the exercises you want to perform.
  4. Track Your Sets
    For each exercise:
    • Enter the weight used
    • Enter the number of reps completed
    • Tap the checkmark to complete the set
    • The rest timer will automatically start
  5. Rest Timer
    After completing a set, a rest timer will start automatically. You can customize rest times in your workout settings. The timer will notify you when rest is complete.
  6. Complete Your Workout
    When finished, tap "End Workout" to save. Your workout will be automatically saved to your history with all statistics calculated.
‚è±Ô∏è

Rest Timer Features

  • Automatic Timer
    The rest timer starts automatically after completing each set. Default rest time is 90 seconds, but you can customize this per exercise.
  • Custom Rest Times
    When creating a workout template or AI plan, you can set custom rest times for each exercise based on your training style.
  • Notifications
    You'll receive a notification when your rest period is complete, so you can focus on recovery without watching the timer.

Generate Your First Plan

  1. Go to Plans Tab
    Tap the "Plans" tab at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Start Plan Creation
    Tap "Create Your First Plan" to begin the guided setup process.
  3. Choose Your Goal
    Select your primary fitness goal:
    • Strength Building
    • Muscle Building
    • Weight Loss
    • Endurance & Cardio
    • Athletic Performance
    • General Fitness
  4. Set Your Preferences
    Configure your plan:
    • Difficulty Level: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
    • Focus Areas: Select muscle groups you want to target
    • Equipment: Choose what equipment you have available
    • Workout Duration: How long you want each workout to be
    • Workouts Per Week: Your training frequency
    • Diet Preferences: Vegetarian, Vegan, Keto, etc.
  5. Review & Generate
    Review your selections and tap "Generate Plan". The AI will create a personalized workout and nutrition plan tailored to your goals.
  6. Start Your Plan
    Once generated, you can view your weekly schedule, workout details, and nutrition plan. Tap "Start Workout" to begin your first session.

Regenerating Plans

You can regenerate parts of your plan if you want to try different workouts or nutrition options.

  1. Access Plan Options
    In the Plans tab, tap the three-dot menu (‚ãØ) on your active plan.
  2. Choose What to Regenerate
    You can regenerate:
    • Workout Plan only
    • Nutrition Plan only
    • Full Plan (both workouts and nutrition)
  3. Regeneration Limits
    Each plan comes with 3 total regenerations (any combination). This allows you to explore different options while maintaining plan quality.

‚ùì

Common Issues & Solutions

HealthKit Not Syncing

Solution: Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Health ‚Üí RepTrack Pro and ensure all permissions are enabled. Make sure you've granted both "Read" and "Write" permissions.

Workouts Not Appearing

Solution: Make sure you've tapped "End Workout" to save your session. If workouts still don't appear, try pulling down to refresh on the History tab.

AI Plan Generation Failing

Solution: Check your internet connection. AI plan generation requires an active internet connection. If the issue persists, try regenerating the plan or contact support.

CloudKit Sync Issues

Solution: Ensure you're signed in to iCloud on your device. Go to Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud and make sure iCloud Drive is enabled. Also check that RepTrack Pro has iCloud permissions enabled.

Widget Not Updating

Solution: Widgets update periodically. If your widget seems outdated, try removing and re-adding it to your Home Screen. Widgets typically update within a few minutes of completing a workout.

App Crashes or Freezes

Solution:

  1. Force close the app and reopen it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Ensure you're running the latest version of iOS
  4. If the issue persists, contact support with details about when the crash occurs
