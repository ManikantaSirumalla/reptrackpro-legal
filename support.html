Quick Start

Getting Started

Set up your profile and start tracking your fitness journey in minutes.

01

Create Your Profile

  1. 1
    Launch the App

    Open RepTrack Pro on your iPhone or iPad. If this is your first time, you'll see the welcome screen.

  2. 2
    Set Up Your Profile

    Tap "Get Started" and fill in your information:

    • Your name and age
    • Height and weight
    • Fitness goal (strength, muscle building, weight loss, etc.)
    • Experience level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
    • Available equipment
  3. 3
    Enable HealthKit

    Allow RepTrack Pro to access your Apple Health data for comprehensive tracking and insights.

  4. 4
    You're Ready!

    Start tracking your first workout or generate an AI-powered fitness plan.

Core Feature

Workout Tracking

Track every set, rep, and weight with precision. Review your performance and see your progress over time.

WT

Start a Workout

  1. 1
    Navigate to Workouts Tab

    Tap the "Workouts" tab at the bottom of the screen.

  2. 2
    Choose Your Workout

    You can:

    • Start a quick workout (add exercises on the fly)
    • Use a workout template (Push/Pull/Legs, Full Body, etc.)
    • Start from an AI-generated plan
  3. 3
    Add Exercises

    Tap "Add Exercise" to browse the exercise library or search for specific exercises. Select the exercises you want to perform.

  4. 4
    Track Your Sets

    For each exercise:

    • Enter the weight used
    • Enter the number of reps completed
    • Tap the checkmark to complete the set
    • The rest timer will automatically start
  5. 5
    Complete Your Workout

    When finished, tap "End Workout" to save. Your workout will be automatically saved to your history with all statistics calculated.

RT

Rest Timer

  1. 1
    Automatic Timer

    The rest timer starts automatically after completing each set. Default rest time is 90 seconds.

  2. 2
    Custom Rest Times

    Set custom rest times for each exercise based on your training style.

  3. 3
    Notifications

    You'll receive a notification when your rest period is complete.

LA

Live Activities iOS 16+

On iPhone 14 Pro and later, your active workout appears on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island for quick access without opening the app.

Pro Tip

Simply start a workout and it will automatically appear on your Lock Screen. Swipe to see your current exercise, sets completed, and rest timer.

AI-Powered Fitness Plans AI

Get personalized workout and nutrition plans generated specifically for your goals and preferences.

AI

Generate Your First Plan

  1. 1
    Go to Plans Tab

    Tap the "Plans" tab at the bottom of the screen.

  2. 2
    Start Plan Creation

    Tap "Create Your First Plan" to begin the guided setup process.

  3. 3
    Choose Your Goal

    Select your primary fitness goal:

    • Strength Building
    • Muscle Building
    • Weight Loss
    • Endurance & Cardio
    • Athletic Performance
    • General Fitness
  4. 4
    Set Your Preferences

    Configure your plan:

    • Difficulty Level: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
    • Focus Areas: Select muscle groups you want to target
    • Equipment: Choose what equipment you have available
    • Workout Duration: How long you want each workout to be
    • Workouts Per Week: Your training frequency
    • Diet Preferences: Vegetarian, Vegan, Keto, etc.
  5. 5
    Review & Generate

    Review your selections and tap "Generate Plan". The AI will create a personalized workout and nutrition plan tailored to your goals.

Regenerating Plans

You can regenerate parts of your plan if you want to try different workouts or nutrition options.

  1. 1

    In the Plans tab, tap the three-dot menu (â‹¯) on your active plan.

Troubleshooting

Quick solutions to common issues you might encounter.

TS

Common Issues & Solutions

HealthKit Not Syncing

Go to Settings â†’ Privacy & Security â†’ Health â†’ RepTrack Pro and ensure all permissions are enabled. Make sure you've granted both "Read" and "Write" permissions.

Workouts Not Appearing

Make sure you've tapped "End Workout" to save your session. If workouts still don't appear, try pulling down to refresh on the History tab.

AI Plan Generation Failing

Check your internet connection. AI plan generation requires an active internet connection. If the issue persists, try regenerating the plan or contact support.

CloudKit Sync Issues

Ensure you're signed in to iCloud on your device. Go to Settings â†’ [Your Name] â†’ iCloud and make sure iCloud Drive is enabled. Also check that RepTrack Pro has iCloud permissions enabled.

Widget Not Updating

Widgets update periodically. If your widget seems outdated, try removing and re-adding it to your Home Screen. Widgets typically update within a few minutes of completing a workout.

App Crashes or Freezes

Try these steps: (1) Force close the app and reopen it, (2) Restart your device, (3) Ensure you're running the latest version of iOS, (4) If the issue persists, contact support with details about when the crash occurs.
